Our Journey

Founded in 2016, Sri Durga Travels began with a simple mission: to provide safe, reliable, and comfortable intercity transportation for travelers in Andhra Pradesh. What started as a single vehicle service has grown into a trusted fleet of premium AC cabs serving thousands of customers annually.

Our founder, with over a decade of experience in the transportation industry, recognized the need for professional cab services that prioritize passenger safety and comfort. Every vehicle in our fleet is equipped with modern amenities, and our drivers undergo rigorous training to ensure the highest standards of service.

Today, we proudly serve passengers traveling between Vijayawada, Hyderabad, Tirupati, and Vizag, making intercity travel in South India safer and more convenient than ever before.
